about-us Notice Board App Notice Board App has been developed for the Department of Engineering, Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering, Pune.

This app provides an individual platform for the institute to share the official notices with its students.

Notices will be well classified into categories like Academic, Scholarship, Assignments and others. So, maintaining them and referring them in the future will become easier. Developed By - Suyash Sonawane Tejas Morkar
